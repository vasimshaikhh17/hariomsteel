Flange Nuts

Flange nuts have a built-in washer-like flange that distributes pressure evenly over the part being secured. This design reduces the risk of damage to the material and provides a secure, vibration-resistant fastening.

  • Integrated flange for even pressure distribution
  • Reduces the need for a separate washer
  • Provides a strong and secure hold
  • Resistant to loosening from vibration
  • Commonly used in automotive and industrial applications
