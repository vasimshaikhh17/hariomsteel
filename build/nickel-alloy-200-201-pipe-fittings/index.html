Nickel UNS N02200 Elbow Pipe Fittings are a solution-heated (2100° F / 1150° C), controlled-carbon alloy with better elevated temperature characteristics. Extruded pipes for ethylene and steam methane reforming furnaces, as well as ammonia effluent refrigerators, are also made from this steel. It is resistant to salt stress-corrosion fracture. Nickel WNR 2.4066 Tee Pipe Fittings have better creep and stress-rupture properties in the temperature range of 1100 degrees Fahrenheit (593 degrees Celsius) to 1800 degrees Fahrenheit (982 degrees Celsius).
