Heavy Hex Nuts

Heavy hex nuts are larger and thicker than standard hex nuts, offering greater strength and load-bearing capacity. They are typically used in heavy-duty applications where extra durability is required.

  • Thicker and wider than standard hex nuts
  • Designed for high-strength and heavy-duty applications
  • Commonly used in structural and industrial projects
  • Provides superior load distribution
  • Made from high-strength materials
