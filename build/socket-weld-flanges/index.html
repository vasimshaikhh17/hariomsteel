Socket Weld Flanges

Hariom Steels is a distinguished supplier of Socket Weld Pipe Flanges, which is a type of austenitic steel with intermediate to high corrosion protection. Alloy 20 Socket Weld Flanges is a high alloyed, acid-proof steel that has been created to withstand corrosion in dilute sulphuric acid. This is super austenitic steel with intermediate to high corrosion protection in a variety of industrial environments. It is high-alloy austenitic steel designed for use in the process sector under extreme corrosion circumstances. It is austenitic steel designed for use under extreme corrosion conditions, with excellent resilience to acidic assaults such as sulphuric, phosphoric, and acetic acid. This is an austenitic steel standard with intermediate to high corrosion resistance, and its high alloy makeup provides excellent resistance to pitting and corrosion in a variety of settings.
